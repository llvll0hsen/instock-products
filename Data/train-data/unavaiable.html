Product Description

The Caterpillar Pull Back Machine Construction Bulldozer is the perfect toy to spark your children's imaginations. What better way to play-pretend to be out and about on the construction site, digging up dirt and clearing it out for that brand new building they're putting in. Your child and their child friends will really enjoy playing outside in real dirt for the ultimate digging experience, or even just play-pretend with their surroundings inside. Whether inside or outside, we know for sure your children are going to have a blast! The CAT Junior Bulldozer is ready for you to take home to your kids! Take it right out of the box, and hand it over, it's time to play. This toy does not require any assembly or need batteries, so it's perfectly convenient, too. Caution is necessary for really small children, as this toy does contain small parts. Bulldozer is ideal for children at least 2 years old, and super fun for children around age 3. They are going to love the bright yellow color of their brand new bulldozer, and they'll know it's a CAT model, thanks to the decal stickers on the side of the toy. It's time to let your kids pick up real or imaginary dirt, and get to having fun with their CAT Pull Back Machine Construction Bulldozer!
