Amazing, fixed my camera on the kitchen table
By ILoveWood on May 22, 2013
Verified Purchase
This is the buy of the century. I replace the display on my Nikon on my kitchen table. BE WARNED, you must be very careful, working slowly and methodically to get everything back together. I lucked out on the getting the stamped steel bezels back together. Go slow, take disassembly photos, label parts with tape, use an egg carton to separate small parts, have good lighting, optivisor if needed. It can be done, but I wouldn't call it trival. Good luck
