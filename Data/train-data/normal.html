SCR-68B Portable Stereo Cassette Player with AM/FM Radio & Earbuds

by JENSEN

Carry the JENSEN SCR-68B AM/FM Stereo Cassette Player or use the built-in belt clip and take it almost anywhere. Whether you're listening to books on tape, purchasing vintage cassette tapes, or you've just revisited a collection of your old mix tapes this handheld stereo cassette player is perfect for you. Weighing in at only a few ounces the SCR-68B is lightweight and portable. It comes with several functions, like play, fast forward, stop, and auto stop. Listen to your favorite radio stations, stereo music, or cassette tapes with the included stereo earbuds.

  • Play, Fast Forward and Stop
  • Auto Stop
  • Stereo Earbuds
  • Detachable Belt Clip
  • Power: 2 x ‘AA’ (UM-3) (Not included) 120V AC Adaptor (AC Adaptor not included)

TWO problems right out of the box (and how to easily fix 'em)
By DB on July 6, 2014
Package Type: Standard Packaging Verified Purchase 16 of 18 people found this review helpful
For the money, its fine. But right out of the packaging, cassettes played horribly. Having worked on cassette machines of all types and sizes, I knew right away what the problem was. The little 'flap' that pushes the cassette towards the playback head wasn't pushing the tape close enough to be played properly. That was fixed by folding up a little piece of paper and placing it between the top of the cassette and the 'flap'. Tapes play fine now.

Secondly (again, right out of the packaging), the tapes played noticeably too fast. So, THAT was fixed by having to unscrew the back of the player and using a mini screwdriver to turn the tape speed adjustment dial.

So, just a heads up on what happened in my case. But again, for the money, it's a fine player (once its working properly).
